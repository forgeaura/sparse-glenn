How to Play Switch

The Goal

Be the first to get rid of all your cards. If another player wins, you get points based on the cards left in your hand (lower is better!).

Basic Rules

You can play a card if it matches the Suit or the Rank of the top card on the pile.

Special Cards

  • 2: Next player picks up 2 cards (unless they have a 2 to stack!).
  • 3: Skips the next player's turn.
  • Joker: Wildcard! Play it anytime to change the suit.
